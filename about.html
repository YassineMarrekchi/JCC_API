What JeyCC Offers: Enhancing Your Festival Experience

JeyCC provides specialized services for the Carthage Film Festival (JCC), including ticketing, transportation, and event management. Our ticketing system offers easy access to JCC. For transportation, we provide carpooling for individuals and CinematDour (cinemobile) and private buses that can be rent for groups and organizations, ensuring convenient and coordinated travel for attendees. Our event management services handle all the details, from preordering Tunisian snacks to organizing processes, creating a smooth and enjoyable experience for all.

With JeyCC, enjoy secure transportation that ensures a safe and comfortable journey, whether you're traveling via CinematDour or our private buses. Reserve your seat effortlessly through our easy ticketing system, securing your place for the exclusive events.

As you immerse yourself in the cinematic experience, indulge in the flavors of Tunisia by preordering traditional snacks to enjoy during the film, adding a delicious cultural touch to your outing. Every detail is thoughtfully designed to create a seamless and memorable experience for all festival-goers.
