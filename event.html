ABOUT JCC

The Carthage Film Festival (JCC) is not just an event; it is a vibrant celebration of culture, creativity, and cinematic excellence that welcomes people from all over the world. Hosted in the heart of Tunis, Tunisia, this prestigious festival has been a beacon for filmmakers, artists, and movie enthusiasts since its inception in 1966. The JCC is more than a showcase of films; it is a cultural journey that highlights the rich tapestry of African and Arab storytelling, offering a platform for emerging and established voices from these regions to share their unique perspectives with a global audience.

The event features a wide array of activities, including film screenings, workshops, panel discussions, and networking opportunities, all designed to immerse participants in the magic of storytelling. Whether you are an aspiring filmmaker seeking inspiration, a cinephile eager to explore groundbreaking films, or simply a culture lover looking for an unforgettable experience, the JCC has something for everyone.

The JCC is a festival where art meets history, where stories transcend boundaries, and where culture becomes a universal language. Join us at the Carthage Film Festival, and be part of an extraordinary event that celebrates the power of cinema to unite, inspire, and transform.

Beyond the cinematic wonders, the festival also provides a gateway to Tunisia's cultural heritage.
