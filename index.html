JCC 2024: Palestine at the Heart of the Avenue

23 November 2024

A selection of 18 films, including feature and short films, directed by Palestinian filmmakers as well as Italian, French, British, and Egyptian directors, is part of the program for the section Palestine at the Heart of the Avenue.
